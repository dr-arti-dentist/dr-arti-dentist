## 🚀 About Me
I am a dental surgeon bridging clinical dentistry and AI research. Using my clinical experience from oral surgery, prosthodontics, and restorative care, I curate clinician-grade annotated dental datasets and train YOLO-based computer vision models to automate pathology detection on dental X-rays (OPGs).
